WhY LOVE RUNNING WITH US?

Feel Healthy

Since it improves aerobic fitness, running is a great tway to improve cardiovascular health. Plus it burns calories and can improve strength.

Feel Strong

Running strenthens your heart by pumping blood more effectively through your body.

Feel Free

Physical exercise, running included, is known to release endorphins in the brain. Endorphins make you more awake, more energetic, and yes, happier.

Get Social

When we run with others we run further and faster more easily. Running socially can pull you out of a low mood and inspire you to try new events.
